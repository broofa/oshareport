The Unofficial OSHA COVID-19 Complaint Form

A form for submitting complaints to OSHA about businesses that are not complying with Oregon COVID-19 restrictions.

This form is not maintained by OSHA (hence, "unofficial"), but it works with their system. "Submit" sends your complaint directly to them, exactly as if you had used the official form. (It's just a lot easier to use!)
